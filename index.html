Our Story

A Retreat Built Into
the Mountainside

Set at the edge of a deodar forest above Landour, Mountain Stay was built on a simple idea — that a mountain holiday should feel unhurried. Eighteen rooms, a handful of fireplaces, and views that change with every hour of light.

Local stone, reclaimed wood, and warm brass fittings carry the character of old Mussoorie, while the comforts inside — underfloor heating, deep soaking tubs, curated minibars — are entirely modern. We built a small hotel on purpose, so that every guest is one we remember.
